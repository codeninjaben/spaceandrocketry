Space And Rocketry

Soyuz

Russia's soyuz rocket has three stages

Electron

The electron is a small and cheap two stage rocket

Delta IV heavy

The delta IV heavy is a two stage rocket made by the United Launch Alliance (ULA)

Falcon 9

The falcon 9 is a reusable two stage rocket meant to go to Low Earth Orbit (LEO)

SLS

The SLS is a two stage rocket capable of going to the moon or even mars

Falcon heavy

The falcon heavy is basically the falcon 9 with two falcon 9 first stage boosters strapped on two it.

Interesting Facts about Space

  1. Space does not begin at a specific altitude above the Earth, but the Karman line at 100 km is a commonly used definition.
  2. The temperature in the void of space is about -270.45° C.
  3. Space is a hard vacuum, meaning it is a void containing very little matter.
  4. There is no sound in space because molecules are too far apart to transmit sound.
  5. The space between galaxies is not completely empty but has an average of one atom per cubic meter.
  6. There are an estimated 100-400 billion stars in our galaxy, the Milky Way.
  7. The universe is observed to be 13.8 billion years old and has been expanding since its formation in the Big Bang.
  8. In the observable universe there are an estimated 2 trillion (2,000,000,000,000) galaxies.
  9. The International Space Station is the largest ever crewed object in space.
  10. Spacecraft have visited all the known planets in our solar system.
